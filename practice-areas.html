Conveyancy

Legal trasfer of property from one owner to another. The process involves a convenyancing solicitor or licenced convenyancer who acts on behalf of the buyer (us) to ensure their client receive the title deed to the property and the land it sits on.

Commercial Law

Its the body of law that applies to the rights, relations and conduct of persons and businesses engaged in commerce. It is often considered to be a branch of civil law and deal with issues of both private law and public law.

Litigation

It refers to actions contested in court; this involves a claim, a dispute or a conflict and use of specific institution, the court to resolve conflict and dispute.

Land Law

It's the form of land that deals with the right to use, alienate or exclude others from land. In many jurisdiction these kind of property are reffered to as real estate or real property.

Family Law

It's a legal practice area that focusses on issues involving family relationships such as marriage, adoption, divorce and child custody among others.

Legal Consultancy Services

Legal consultants are attoneys hired by law firms to assist with particular case or legal matter. They are often experts in specific field of law.
